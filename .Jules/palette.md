## 2026-05-03 - Icon-only buttons missing aria labels
**Learning:** Found multiple instances of icon-only buttons (like edit and delete buttons in lists) missing `aria-label` and `title` attributes. This is a common accessibility issue that needs to be addressed across the app to ensure screen reader users can understand button actions.
**Action:** Always verify icon-only buttons have descriptive `aria-label` and `title` attributes when adding new ones or updating existing components.
